I am Psychologist and Researcher at the Hasso Plattner Institute specialized in Behavior Design. Foremost, I am intrueged by the question: How can we design and evaluate technology to guide people in their decision-making journeys?

In my PhD, I investigated interventions aimed at energy and water conservation practices in every day life. My approach involves translating behavioral insights into effective socio-technological systems, prioritizing both user needs and organizational challenges to create practical solutions. Beyond designing, I test these systems through interviews and experiments to validate their effectiveness.

Outside the lab, I engage in teaching, coaching, and conducting workshops for professionals, sharing knowledge and learning from diverse perspectives.

On a personal note, my love for electronic music led me to co-found Menura Audio, where we’re developing an innovative Modular DJ mixer. This project combines my interests in technology and music, reflecting my belief in the power of simple, effective technology to delight our lives.
